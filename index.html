Purpose

Milky's is a Los Angeles based DIY experiential brand and hub that's goal is to cultivate alternative IRL and immersive experiences through music, film and performance. Milky's also lends itself as a modular production studio/location rental with additional office spaces and a private parking lot. Milky's also harbors profound rock producer Will Yip's LA recording studio, The Offices of Talent Mgmt company The Sakai Group and Non-Profit Music Platform ASEMBLI WORLD.
